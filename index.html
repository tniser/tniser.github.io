Tournament info

This is an open-rank tournament, meaning that users that are ranked from Rank 1 to Rank 8 are eligible for this tournament! This is equivalent to Dota 2's Herald to Immortal ranks.


This tournament is played in a best-of-one (BO1), single-elimination format. No entry fee is required. The game mode is 5v5 Captain's Mode.


Our tournaments require four teams to start, to ensure you get the best competitive experience. A tournament containing less than the mentioned amount will automatically be cancelled, and any entry fee will be refunded through our customer support.

General Information

Check-in opens 60 minutes before the tournament starts.


After that, players have 45 minutes to confirm their participation.


Make sure to read and understand the instructions in the articles under ”How it Works” before joining a game.


You are required to use the same Steam account you have synced with your Epulze account.


Each break between matches in a tournament is 5 minutes long.

Join the Game

You will receive a lobby invite through the Dota 2 client or, alternatively through the Epulze pre-game view.


Players have 5 minutes (1v1) or 10 minutes (5v5) to join the in-game lobby.


The game will start automatically when all player slots are full.


Make sure you and your team are in the correct slots.
